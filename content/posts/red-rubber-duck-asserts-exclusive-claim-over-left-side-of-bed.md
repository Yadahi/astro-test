A red rubber duck identified as Kachna has declared sole and irrevocable ownership of the left side of a bed, sources close to the mattress confirmed Monday.

Kachna, who stands approximately 3 inches tall and has held the position since "before anyone can remember," issued no formal statement but communicated her claim through what witnesses described as "an unwavering stare and strategic placement near the good pillow."

"She was just there one morning," said one resident, who asked to remain anonymous out of fear of reprisal. "We assumed it was temporary. That was four years ago."

Attempts to relocate Kachna to the right side of the bed, the nightstand, or a nearby shelf have failed repeatedly. Sources say the duck returns to her preferred position within hours, her expression unchanged, her resolve unbroken.

## Territorial Dispute Escalates

The situation intensified last Tuesday when a second pillow was introduced to the left side, which Kachna reportedly accepted as tribute rather than competition.

"She sat on it immediately," the resident said. "I think she's expanding."

Legal experts say Kachna's claim, while unconventional, may be difficult to challenge. "Possession is nine-tenths of the law," said one attorney, who declined to take the case after learning the client was a duck.

Kachna has not commented on whether she intends to extend her jurisdiction to the left side of the couch, though residents say two throw pillows went missing last week under unexplained circumstances.

As of press time, Kachna remained in position, dry, composed, and entirely unbothered.
